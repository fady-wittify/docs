### What types of agents can I create on Wittify?
You can create three types of AI agents:
- **Voice Agent**: Interacts via voice calls.
- **Text Agent**: Communicates through chat channels like WhatsApp or Instagram.
- **Hybrid Agent**: Combines voice and text interaction into one seamless agent.

### Can I edit an agent after it's created?
Yes, you can go back and update your agent's name, behavior, channels, and integrations at any time via the agent settings panel.

### How can I embed my agent on my website?
Go to the **Deployment** tab of your agent, click **"Embed on Your Website"**, then copy and paste the provided `<iframe>` code into your website HTML. This allows customers to interact with your agent directly from your site.

### What integrations are supported?
Wittify supports integrations with:
- WhatsApp
- Instagram
- Messenger
- Google Calendar
- Custom webhooks
Check the **Integrations** tab for setup instructions.

Use the **Test Agent** button on the top bar. This allows you to simulate interactions before deploying it to a live channel.
